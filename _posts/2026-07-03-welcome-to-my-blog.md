Welcome to my personal blog! I'm Praveen Ezhil — an engineer focused on building distributed systems that are scalable and resilient.

## What to expect here

This is where I'll share thoughts, learnings, and deep dives on topics I care about:

- **Distributed systems** — consensus, replication, fault tolerance, CAP theorem tradeoffs
- **Scalability patterns** — sharding, caching, load balancing, backpressure
- **Reliability engineering** — SLOs, retries, circuit breakers, observability
- **Engineering practice** — lessons learned from building and operating systems at scale

## Why write?

Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain something simply, I don't understand it well enough. This blog is as much for me as it is for anyone reading it.
